Beginner difficulty (I'm Too Young to Die):
1) Guaranted drop from every enemy
2) 3 additional lifes in every attempt
3) Slow turrets and bullets

Medium difficulty (Hurt Me Plenty):
1) 70% chance of drop from every enemy
2) 2 additional lifes in every attempt
3) Normal reaction of turrets and bullets speed

Hard difficulty (Ultra Violence):
1) 35% chance of drop from every enemy
2) 1 additional life in every attempt
3) Very fast reaction of turrets and bullets speed

Insane difficulty (Nightmare):
1) Any drop is absent
2) Additional lifes is absent
3) Perfect reaction of turrets and bullets speed
4) No hope...
